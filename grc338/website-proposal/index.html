Introduction

Cowabunga Coffee

Cowabunga Coffee is a beachside coffee shop with locations in Pismo Beach and Santa Barbara. It has a surfer aesthetic and serves many customers on a daily basis.

Target audience

The two Cowabunga Coffee locations are in college towns, so the primary target audiene is college students.

College students are either looking for a place to sit down and study, or they are looking for a place to grab coffee and catch up with a friend on campus they haven't seen in awhile. College students are often on the go, and may be looking at menus online to prepare for their trip tp get caffine inbetween class

About

We are Nic & Paolo Battaglini, owners of Cowabunga Coffee. We spent over 12 years dreaming, planning, learning and working in all things coffee with the hope that someday we would start a shop of our own. In December of 2001, we launched Cowabunga Coffee by Pismo Beach. In August of 2011, we opened our second Cowabunga Coffee location near UCSB. We now have over 30 wonderful employees, and love that the community has made Cowabunga Coffee one of the most sought after destinations on the California Coast. Thank you so much to all of you who have made this possible. Our goal has always been to create inspiring spaces, with great people and amazing products, in a way that truly adds value to the community around us. We hope you love it here!
